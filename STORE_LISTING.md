## Detailed Description
```
ShortsBlock: Build Focus on YouTube

Regain control of your YouTube experience. ShortsBlock helps you stay focused by removing distractions and algorithmic rabbit holes.

🎯 FEATURES

✅ Block YouTube Shorts
Hide Shorts buttons, shelves, and tabs. Redirect Shorts URLs to your subscriptions.

✅ Redirect Homepage
Automatically redirect YouTube homepage to your subscriptions feed — skip the algorithm.

✅ Grayscale Mode
Turn thumbnails black & white to reduce clickbait appeal and dopamine-driven scrolling.

✅ Hide Metrics
Hide view counts, subscriber counts, publish dates, and like counts — pick videos based on content, not popularity.

✅ Redact Comments
Replace comment text with "blah" to avoid negativity and time-wasting debates.

✅ Focused Watch Page
Hide recommended videos sidebar and end-screen recommendations for distraction-free viewing.

⚙️ GRANULAR CONTROL

Every feature can be toggled independently. Use only what you need:
- Master on/off toggle (pause the extension anytime)
- 5 independent feature toggles
- Settings sync across devices (Chrome Sync)
- Real-time updates across all YouTube tabs

🔒 PRIVACY

ShortsBlock does NOT collect, transmit, or share any data. All settings stay on your device. Read our privacy policy: https://github.com/meiyo99/shortsblock/blob/main/PRIVACY.md

📦 LIGHTWEIGHT & FAST

- No performance impact
- Instant enable/disable
- Works on all YouTube pages
- Compatible with Brave, Edge, and Chrome

🎨 BEAUTIFUL DESIGN

Clean, native-feeling popup with dark theme and premium toggle animations.

---

Built for intentional YouTube usage. Focus on what matters.
```
